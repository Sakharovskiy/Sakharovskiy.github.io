› What is the QuietLand?

This is a private server, close to vanilla Minecraft with a bunch of features that will not let any player get bored. On our project you can create a kingdom or city, take an active part in politics and create your own events, trade with other players, get an interesting government job, and much more! Add to this several private plugins and datapacks that do not break the vanilla, but only diversify the gameplay - this is all QuietLand!
